DID YOU KNOW?
Anne Hathaway really wanted to be a nun??
It was her childhood dream!!
But she also had a passion for acting!!

RISING TO FAME

Passionate with Music and Acting as a kid, she joined multiple plays in school.
And in 2001, she landed her first role as Mia Thermopolis in The Princess Diaries, one of the most iconic 2000s movies of all time!!
And it didn't stop there!
She starred in Ella Enchanted in 2004, Devil Wears Prada in 2006, Rachel Getting Married in 2008, Bride Wars in 2009, Les Miserables and The Dark Knight Rises in 2012, Interstellar in 2014, and Ocean's 8 in 2018!
She Became such an icon!!

A WOMAN NOT BLINDED BY FAME

Though Anne remains as one of the most iconic
people in Hollywood, she didn't let such fame blind her.
This truly pure hearted woman is an ambassador of the UN, supporting women around the world,
and providing education to those in need of it!!
